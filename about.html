Our Foundation

Clarity at the Critical Moments

The Torat Habayit Institute was established to address one of the most pressing needs of modern Jewish life: providing authoritative, practical, and compassionate halachic guidance when medicine and Torah intersect.

When a patient faces a life-altering medical decision, when a doctor must navigate a patient's religious observance, or when a family confronts questions no one prepared them for — the Institute is there.

We serve as a trusted bridge: rigorous in our scholarship, sensitive in our approach, and always guided by the principle that every halachic decision has profound human consequences.

Biography

A Life Dedicated to Bridging Torah and Medicine

Rabbi Yehuda Finchas is one of the foremost authorities in Medical Halacha in the world today. A graduate of the finest yeshivot and a practising posek for over three decades, Rabbi Finchas founded the Torat Habayit Institute with a singular vision: that every Jew deserves authoritative, accessible Torah guidance at the most vulnerable moments of life.

His responsa span the most complex areas of modern medicine — from the halachic status of brain death and organ donation, to the permissibility of assisted reproductive technologies, to end-of-life nutrition and hydration. He is consulted by leading Batei Din across Israel, the United States, the United Kingdom, and beyond.

Rabbi Finchas is equally at home delivering a shiur to a gathering of senior physicians at Hadassah Medical Center as he is providing personal counsel to a family in crisis. His approach is defined by two qualities rarely found together: uncompromising halachic rigour and profound human compassion.

He lectures internationally at hospitals, medical schools, synagogues, and yeshivot — bringing the voice of halacha into every environment where its guidance is needed.
