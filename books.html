Published Works by Torat Habayit

Over twelve volumes of rigorous Medical-Halachic research — essential references for rabbis, physicians, and scholars.

Volume I

Nutrition and Hydration in Halacha

A comprehensive examination of the halachic issues surrounding artificial nutrition and hydration (ANH) in end-of-life care. This landmark volume provides the framework for rabbis and medical professionals navigating some of the most emotionally and spiritually challenging decisions a family can face.

Topics include: the status of a gosés, when withholding nutrition is permitted, the halachic view of palliative care, and the obligations of physicians under Jewish law.

Volume II (English)

Brain Death in Halacha

One of the most complex and urgent debates in contemporary Halacha — this volume presents a thorough analysis of the halachic status of brain death and its direct implications for organ donation. Written for both halachic authorities and medical professionals.

Covers: definitions of death in halachic literature, clinical criteria for brain death, the organ donation controversy, and the rulings of major contemporary poskim.

Volume IV

Medicine on Chagim & Shabbat

A practical halachic guide for both patients and medical staff — covering the permissibility of medications, medical procedures, and hospital visits on Shabbat and Yom Tov. An indispensable resource for every observant Jewish home and every Jewish medical professional.

Topics include: taking medications on fast days, travelling to hospital on Yom Tov, the laws of pikuach nefesh in practice, and managing chronic conditions on Shabbat.
